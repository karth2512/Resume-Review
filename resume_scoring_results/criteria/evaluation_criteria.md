# Evaluation Criteria: Senior Software Engineer - Full Stack

This document contains the structured evaluation criteria extracted from the job description.

## Required Technical Skills

### JavaScript proficiency

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for JavaScript listed in skills section, project descriptions using JavaScript, or work experience involving JavaScript development. Should show strong/advanced level usage.

---

### TypeScript proficiency

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check for TypeScript in skills or tech stack, projects built with TypeScript, or explicit mention in work experience. Look for evidence of type-safe development practices.

---

### Modern web frameworks (React, Vue, or Angular)

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Identify specific framework mentions (React, Vue, or Angular) in skills or project sections. Look for component-based development, state management, and framework-specific patterns in work descriptions.

---

### Backend development (Node.js, Python, or Java)

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for at least one of Node.js, Python, or Java in backend/server-side development context. Check for API development, server-side logic, or backend architecture experience.

---

### RESTful APIs understanding

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for mentions of REST API design, development, or consumption. Check for terms like endpoint development, API integration, HTTP methods, or REST principles.

---

### Microservices architecture knowledge

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Search for microservices, distributed systems, or service-oriented architecture in experience. Look for descriptions of building or working with independent, loosely-coupled services.

---

### SQL databases experience (PostgreSQL or similar)

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Identify SQL database mentions like PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server. Look for database design, query optimization, or relational database management in work history.

---

### NoSQL databases experience (MongoDB or similar)

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check for NoSQL databases like MongoDB, Cassandra, DynamoDB, or Redis. Look for document stores, key-value databases, or non-relational data modeling experience.

---

### Git version control

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for Git, GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket mentions. Check for branching strategies, pull requests, code collaboration, or version control workflows in experience.

---

### CI/CD pipelines experience

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Search for CI/CD, continuous integration, continuous deployment, or specific tools like Jenkins, GitHub Actions, CircleCI, Travis CI. Look for automated testing and deployment pipeline creation.

---

### Cloud platform experience (AWS, Azure, or GCP)

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Identify cloud platform mentions (AWS, Azure, GCP). Look for specific services used (EC2, S3, Lambda, Azure Functions, etc.) or cloud infrastructure management experience.

---

### Containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes)

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for Docker, Kubernetes, container orchestration, or containerization in skills or projects. Check for Dockerfile creation, K8s deployments, or container management experience.

---

## Preferred Technical Skills

### Agile/Scrum methodologies experience

**Weight:** Medium  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for mentions of Agile, Scrum, sprint planning, stand-ups, retrospectives, or Scrum Master/Product Owner collaboration. Check for iterative development processes.

---

### DevOps practices familiarity

**Weight:** Medium  
**Evaluation Guide:** Search for DevOps, infrastructure as code, automation, monitoring, or related tools (Terraform, Ansible, Prometheus). Look for development and operations integration experience.

---

### Open-source contributions

**Weight:** Low  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check for GitHub profile links, mentions of open-source projects contributed to, pull requests to public repositories, or maintained OSS projects. Look for community involvement.

---

## Experience Requirements

### 5+ years of professional software development experience

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Calculate total years of professional software development experience from work history. Ensure at least 5 years in software engineering roles, excluding internships unless specified otherwise.

---

### Experience mentoring junior developers

**Weight:** Medium  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for explicit mentions of mentoring, coaching, training junior developers, or leading team members. Check for descriptions of code review leadership or knowledge sharing activities.

---

### Proven track record of delivering high-quality software solutions

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Assess through project outcomes, successful product launches, scalability achievements, or measurable impact. Look for quality indicators like testing practices, code standards, or production stability mentions.

---

## Education Requirements

### Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

**Weight:** Medium  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check education section for Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or related technical fields. Note: Listed as preferred, so equivalent experience may substitute.

---

## Soft Skills

### Strong problem-solving skills

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for descriptions of complex technical challenges solved, troubleshooting experience, or optimization achievements. Check for analytical thinking, debugging expertise, or innovative solutions in work history.

---

### Attention to detail

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Assess through mentions of code quality, testing rigor, bug prevention, code review thoroughness, or precision in technical work. Look for quality assurance involvement or error reduction achievements.

---

### Strong communication skills

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for collaboration with cross-functional teams, technical documentation, presenting to stakeholders, or explaining technical concepts. Check for client-facing roles or team coordination experience.

---

### Teamwork skills

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Identify collaborative project work, cross-functional team participation, pair programming, or team-based achievements. Look for phrases indicating working with designers, product managers, or other engineers.

---

### Mentoring and leadership abilities

**Weight:** Medium  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check for experience conducting code reviews, leading technical discussions, onboarding team members, or guiding architectural decisions. Look for leadership in technical direction without formal management role.

---

## Domain Knowledge

### Web application development expertise

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for experience building web applications, SaaS products, or web-based platforms. Check for full-stack development, responsive design, browser technologies, and web performance optimization.

---

### Scalable architecture design

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Search for experience with high-traffic systems, performance optimization, load balancing, caching strategies, or architecture for growth. Look for mentions of handling increased user loads or scaling challenges.

---

## Other Requirements

### Ability to write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code

**Weight:** Critical  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for mentions of code quality standards, unit testing, integration testing, TDD/BDD practices, code documentation, or adherence to design patterns and best practices.

---

### Code review experience

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Check for explicit mentions of conducting code reviews, peer review processes, providing technical feedback, or ensuring code quality standards across the team.

---

### Participation in technical architecture decisions

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Look for involvement in system design, architecture planning, technology selection, or technical strategy. Check for descriptions of designing solutions, evaluating trade-offs, or influencing technical direction.

---

### Production troubleshooting and issue resolution

**Weight:** High  
**Evaluation Guide:** Search for on-call experience, production support, debugging live systems, incident response, or performance troubleshooting. Look for mentions of maintaining system reliability or resolving critical issues.
